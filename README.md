# 🍽️ AI-Driven Restaurant Decision Intelligence System

## 📌 Project Overview
This project is a data-driven decision intelligence system designed to help restaurants optimize pricing, forecast demand, and target customers effectively using machine learning and analytics.

The system integrates descriptive, diagnostic, predictive, and prescriptive analytics to improve revenue, reduce food wastage, and enhance operational efficiency.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Predict customer demand patterns  
- Optimize pricing strategies  
- Segment customers based on behavior  
- Identify popular menu combinations  
- Provide actionable business recommendations  

---

## 🧠 Key Features

### 📊 1. Descriptive Analytics
- Demand trends by time slot  
- Customer distribution and spending patterns  

---

### 🔍 2. Diagnostic Analytics
- Identify factors influencing customer decisions  
- Analyze price sensitivity and discount impact  

---

### 🤖 3. Predictive Analytics
- Regression Model → Predict demand (total_orders_hour)  
- Classification Model → Predict purchase intent  

#### Metrics Used:
- Accuracy  
- Precision  
- Recall  
- F1 Score  
- ROC Curve  

---

### 👥 4. Customer Segmentation
- K-Means Clustering  
- Identifies Budget, Regular, and Premium customers  

---

### 🛒 5. Association Rule Mining
- Identifies frequently bought item combinations  
- Metrics: Support, Confidence, Lift  

---

### 🚀 6. Prescriptive Analytics
The system recommends:
- Optimal pricing strategies  
- Targeted discounts  
- Product bundling strategies  

---

### 📂 7. New Customer Prediction
- Upload new customer dataset  
- Predict purchase intent  
- Enable targeted marketing strategies  

---

## 📊 Dataset
- Synthetic dataset with 2000+ customers  
- Generated using behavioral and probabilistic modeling  
- Includes demographics, spending behavior, price sensitivity, demand patterns, and experimental responses
